Anthropic was founded in 2021 by former OpenAI researchers with a stated focus on AI safety research. The company develops the Claude series of models, which are available through API and the Claude.ai consumer product. Anthropic received a C+ overall grade on the FLI AI Safety Index (Winter 2025), the highest score among companies assessed, though no company scored above D on existential safety.
