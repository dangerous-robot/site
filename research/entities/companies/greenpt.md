GreenPT is an employee-owned AI chatbot that runs exclusively on open-weight models (Mistral Small, Mistral Large) hosted by Infomaniak on Swiss renewable energy infrastructure. The service provides per-call energy and emissions data through its API. GreenPT operates under Swiss data protection law (FADP + GDPR) and does not use customer data for model training.
