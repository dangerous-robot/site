Ecosia is a Berlin-based search engine that directs revenue toward tree-planting projects. The company is structured as steward-ownership ("Verantwortungseigentum"), meaning it cannot be sold and profits are permanently committed to its mission. Ecosia added an AI chat feature powered by GPT-4 mini, which runs on OpenAI/Azure infrastructure rather than Ecosia's own servers.
