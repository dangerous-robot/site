Multiple independent sources confirm that Microsoft contributes financially to environmental initiatives. The Nature Conservancy reports a partnership in which Microsoft funds projects to restore watersheds, protect biodiversity, and develop conservation tools, aiming to replenish more water than it consumes and protect more land than it uses by 2030【2026/microsoft-nature-conservancy】.
