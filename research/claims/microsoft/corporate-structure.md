Microsoft is a publicly‑traded corporation. Independent reporting details that its shares are held by a broad base of institutional investors—including Vanguard (≈8.9% of outstanding shares) and BlackRock—and by individual shareholders such as Steve Ballmer and Bill Gates, confirming that Microsoft’s equity is listed on public stock exchanges and that it follows a publicly‑traded corporate structure. No sources indicate that Microsoft is privately‑held, a non‑profit, a B‑Corp, employee‑owned, steward‑owned, or a cooperative.
