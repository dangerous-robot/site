According to The Guardian, OpenAI has completed its conversion from a non-profit organization to a for-profit public benefit corporation structure. The restructuring involved over a year of negotiations with Delaware and California attorneys general and resulted in Microsoft receiving a 27% stake valued at $100 billion. While the non-profit entity remains in control of the for-profit corporation, OpenAI now operates under a hybrid structure that enables it to raise capital more easily while maintaining some oversight through its foundational non-profit component.
