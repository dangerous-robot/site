According to sources 2, 3, and 4 from 2026, OpenAI transitioned to a public benefit corporation (PBC) structure in late 2025. Source 2 notes that Microsoft holds 27% of the for-profit entity, while Source 3 identifies current and former employees as owning 25% in total. Source 3 further specifies that OpenAI was valued at $500 billion in 2026. Source 4 highlights the hybrid setup involving the OpenAI Foundation and a for-profit subsidiary. While not entirely privately owned, nor publicly traded on a traditional exchange, the non-profit to PBC transition aligns with a limited-public stake model.}
