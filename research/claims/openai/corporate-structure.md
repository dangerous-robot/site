Independent reporting confirms that OpenAI is organized under a controlling nonprofit entity (the OpenAI Foundation) that oversees a for‑profit public‑benefit corporation. NBC News describes the restructuring into a nonprofit and a for‑profit public benefit corporation, CNN Business notes that OpenAI will remain under nonprofit control, and ProPublica’s tax‑filing data shows OpenAI Inc is registered as a 501(c)(3) nonprofit. These sources together establish that OpenAI’s corporate structure includes a nonprofit component, satisfying the claim that it has a non‑profit corporate structure.
