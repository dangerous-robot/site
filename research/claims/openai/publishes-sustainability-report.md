OpenAI does not publish a sustainability or ESG report according to available evidence. The Sustainable Innovation analysis explicitly states that "OpenAI has no publicly released standalone sustainability report" and describes the company as "among the least transparent in its environmental reporting." This lack of formal sustainability reporting is further evidenced by third-party analysis from DitchCarbon giving OpenAI a climate score of just 23 out of 100, indicating poor environmental transparency and disclosure practices.
