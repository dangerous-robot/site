According to The Sustainable Innovation's comprehensive analysis, OpenAI has not published a formal sustainability or ESG report as of March 2026. The analysis found that OpenAI "remains among the least transparent in its environmental reporting" and noted "the complete absence of a formal emissions reporting framework." DitchCarbon gave OpenAI a climate score of just 23 out of 100, specifically citing "the absence of specific emissions figures" and lack of "formally documented reduction targets." The report also states that "OpenAI has published zero verified Scope 1, 2, or 3 figures as of March 2026," indicating no formal sustainability disclosure.
