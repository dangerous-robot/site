OpenAI provides direct financial support for AI‑safety research through a dedicated fellowship program.  The fellowship offers external researchers a weekly stipend of $3,850 and up to $15,000 per month in compute resources to work on safety, alignment, robustness, and related topics (AI Daily).  Help Net Security reports that OpenAI is accepting applications for this paid external fellowship, which will run from September 2026 through February 2027, and will fund researchers in both technical and social‑science domains (Help Net Security).  THE Journal likewise notes that the six‑month Safety Fellowship is designed to fund external practitioners studying AI‑risk areas such as agentic oversight and high‑severity misuse (THE Journal).  These programs constitute organized funding of AI‑safety causes, confirming that OpenAI does indeed donate resources to the field.
