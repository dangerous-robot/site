The Future of Life Institute’s 2025 AI Safety Index evaluated the leading AI/LLM producers (Anthropic, OpenAI, Google DeepMind, xAI, Meta, Zhipu AI, DeepSeek, etc.) across several safety domains, including Existential Safety planning. All of the reports summarising the index – the primary “2025 AI Safety Index” (Source 1), the Winter 2025 update (Source 2), the Quantum Zeitgeist article (Source 4), and The Guardian coverage (Source 5) – state that **no company scored above a D** in the Existential Safety domain. This directly confirms that the top AI/LLM producers received grades of D or lower for existential‑threat safety.
