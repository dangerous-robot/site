Multiple independent sources confirm that the major developers of large‑language models have formally agreed to voluntary safety commitments aimed at managing the risks of frontier AI. The 2024 AI Seoul Summit produced a set of Frontier AI Safety Commitments that were signed by leading organizations such as OpenAI, Google, Meta, Anthropic, and others【Source 4】. A later report notes that sixteen AI developers, including Microsoft and OpenAI, signed the same commitments, pledging to publish safety frameworks and adopt risk‑threshold policies【Source 7】. AI Lab Watch maintains a public list of such commitments from the top AI labs, further documenting that companies like OpenAI, Anthropic, Google, and Microsoft have publicly pledged to adhere to safety protocols【Source 6】. Together, these sources demonstrate that the leading AI/LLM producers have indeed signed frontier safety commitments.
