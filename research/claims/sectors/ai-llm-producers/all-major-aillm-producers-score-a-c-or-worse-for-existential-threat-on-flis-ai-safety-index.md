The Future of Life Institute’s AI Safety Index explicitly reports that none of the evaluated frontier AI companies received a grade above D in the Existential Safety domain.  The 2025 AI Safety Index states, “none scored above D in Existential Safety planning” (Source 2).  A secondary summary of the same index confirms this, noting, “No company achieved a score above a D in this domain for the second consecutive edition.” (Source 5).  Because a D is lower than a C, these statements together imply that every major AI/LLM producer earned a grade of C or worse (i.e., D, E, or F).  No source provides evidence of any company attaining a C, B, or A in this domain, so the claim is fully supported.
