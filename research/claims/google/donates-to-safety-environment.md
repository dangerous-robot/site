Google provides substantial funding to both AI safety and environmental causes through multiple initiatives. Google.org's Impact Challenge: AI for Science allocates $30M to researchers using AI for health and climate breakthroughs, with individual grants ranging from $500K to $3M. The Google.org Impact Challenge on Climate Innovation committed another $30M to six organizations receiving $5M each plus technical support for climate solutions using AI and digital tools. Additionally, Google Research Award program supports university researchers with up to $100K grants for AI safety, privacy, and security research. These programs demonstrate consistent multi-million dollar commitments to both environmental and AI safety causes.
