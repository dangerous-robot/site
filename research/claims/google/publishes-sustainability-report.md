Google issues an official sustainability/ESG report each year. The most recent is the **2025 Environmental Report**, which is hosted on Google’s sustainability website and provides a downloadable PDF covering fiscal 2024 data and early‑2025 achievements [[2025/google-2025-environmental-report]]. An independent article on CIO.com also notes the publication of Google’s 2023 Environmental Report, confirming that Google regularly releases such documents [[2026/google-environmental-report-2023]]. The ResponsibilityReports.com index lists the latest 2024 Environmental Report and links to the archive of prior reports, showing the public availability of these ESG disclosures [[2026/google-inc]].
