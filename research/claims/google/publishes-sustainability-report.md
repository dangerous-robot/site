Google publishes annual sustainability and ESG reports. The company released its 2025 Environmental Report, which the sources describe as its 10th annual environmental report, covering energy, AI, and resilience with 2024 fiscal year data. Additionally, Google published a 2023 Responsibility Report that explicitly covers ESG performance, sustainability initiatives, governance, human rights, and corporate social responsibility metrics. Both reports are publicly available and represent Google's primary external communications on environmental and social responsibility.
