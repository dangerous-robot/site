Google operates as a publicly-traded company, as evidenced by its IPO process and current corporate structure. According to CNN's reporting, Google went public at $85 per share, raising $1.67 billion through its stock offering. The company's current status as a publicly-traded entity is further confirmed by the Alphabet Investor Relations portal, which serves as the central hub for financial information and earnings data for shareholders and analysts.
