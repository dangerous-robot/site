Google operates as a publicly-traded company under the parent holding company Alphabet Inc. According to the Alphabet Investor Relations announcement, Google shareholders' shares automatically converted to Alphabet shares, which continue trading on Nasdaq under two share classes. The NASDAQ institutional holdings data further confirms that Google (ticker GOOGL) is tracked as a publicly-traded security with institutional investor holdings. This directly satisfies the 'publicly-traded' corporate structure category.
