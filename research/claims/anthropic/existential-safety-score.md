The Future of Life Institute's AI Safety Index (Winter 2025) found that no AI company scored above D on existential safety. Anthropic received the highest overall grade at C+, but still scored poorly on plans for catastrophic or irreversible AI risks. The report assessed 8 major AI companies across 6 safety domains, and every company scored below C on at least one critical domain.
