According to multiple sources, Anthropic is structured as a privately-held public benefit corporation. Revenue Memo reports that Anthropic is "privately-held" with major investors including Amazon ($8 billion) and Google ($2 billion), while maintaining that "no single investor holds a controlling stake." Both Anthropic's own materials and Time confirm the company operates as a "public benefit corporation" with a unique Long-Term Benefit Trust governance structure. While the claim lists multiple possible corporate structures, the evidence clearly supports that Anthropic fits the "privately-held" category, though with the additional designation as a public benefit corporation designed to balance profit with social benefit.
