Anthropic publishes detailed model specifications for Claude through its public documentation. The 'Models overview' source lists specific Claude variants (Opus 4.7, Sonnet 4.6, Haiku 4.5) with pricing, performance metrics, context windows, and capabilities comparisons. The Transparency Hub provides additional technical information and safety evaluations for multiple Claude model versions. However, the 'Anthropic Transparency Report' indicates that most proprietary details regarding model architecture, compute specifications, and internal development processes are withheld 'to protect competitive advantages and intellectual property.' While Anthropic discloses which Claude models exist and their public capabilities, it does not disclose the underlying models or foundational approaches that comprise each Claude version.
