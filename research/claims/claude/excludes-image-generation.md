All three source documents state explicitly that Claude cannot create photos, illustrations, or any other type of image. The Claude Help Center notes that "Claude doesn’t generate photos or illustrations the way image‑generation tools do" and limits its visual output to diagrams and charts built with HTML/SVG. Anthropic’s Vision guide and the Data Studios article both repeat that "Claude cannot generate, produce, edit, manipulate or create images" and that its role is limited to viewing and analyzing uploaded images. Consequently, the claim that Claude excludes image generation is fully supported by the available evidence.
