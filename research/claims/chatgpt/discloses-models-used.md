According to ZDNet's analysis of OpenAI's GPT-4 technical report, OpenAI has moved away from transparency regarding model specifications. The report states that OpenAI's GPT-4 paper 'contains no further details about the architecture (including model size), hardware, training compute, dataset construction, training method, or similar,' making the GPT-4 program 'a complete enigma.' This represents a departure from traditional AI research practices where technical details were typically disclosed.
