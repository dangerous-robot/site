The sources provide estimates of ChatGPT's energy consumption but do not indicate that the ChatGPT service shows users any real‑time energy‑usage data. Business Energy UK’s "ChatGPT Energy Consumption Visualized" estimates daily water and electricity use based on model activity, Epoch AI’s "How much energy does ChatGPT use?" calculates per‑query watt‑hour usage, and IEEE Spectrum’s "How Much Energy Does It Take to Power Billions of AI Queries?" projects aggregate consumption. None of these articles describe a real‑time display feature within ChatGPT. Consequently, the claim that ChatGPT displays real‑time energy usage is unsupported.
