ChatGPT does not currently display real-time energy usage information to users. The "Users Energy Usage Dashboard" source describes a community feature request for an energy usage dashboard that would show environmental impact and real-time energy consumption tracking, indicating this functionality does not exist. While the "How much energy does ChatGPT use?" analysis provides detailed estimates of ChatGPT's energy consumption per query (approximately 0.3 watt-hours for GPT-4o), this research was conducted externally by Epoch AI rather than being displayed within ChatGPT itself.
