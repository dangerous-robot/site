However, none of the articles address whether OpenAI publicly reveals *where* the electricity used by ChatGPT comes from—whether it is sourced from renewable, fossil‑fuel, or mixed grids.  The Guardian piece notes that OpenAI will not disclose GPT‑5’s energy use 【OpenAI will not disclose GPT‑5’s energy use】, but it also does not speak to energy sourcing for ChatGPT itself.  Consequently, the claim that “ChatGPT discloses its energy sourcing” is not directly supported or contradicted by the available evidence.
