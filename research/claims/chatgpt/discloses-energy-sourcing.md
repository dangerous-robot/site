OpenAI provides limited energy consumption information for ChatGPT models. While "Environmental Impact of AI" discusses energy efficiency improvements and cites independent research showing GPT-4o queries consume approximately 0.3 watt-hours, "OpenAI will not disclose GPT-5's energy use" reports that OpenAI has provided no official energy consumption data since GPT-3. The Guardian article specifically notes researchers calling on OpenAI to commit to full transparency by publicly disclosing environmental impact data. The available information appears to come primarily from independent analysis rather than comprehensive disclosure by OpenAI itself.
