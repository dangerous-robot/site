However, none of the examined sources address whether OpenAI publicly reveals the origin of the electricity powering ChatGPT—such as the proportion sourced from renewable versus non‑renewable generation. Because the claim’s central assertion (disclosure of energy sourcing) is not addressed by any source, the claim remains unverified despite extensive coverage of related environmental impacts.
