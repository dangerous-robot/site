The claim that ChatGPT excludes image generation is contradicted by multiple sources. OpenAI’s Images 2.0 model, which is accessed through ChatGPT, is explicitly described as “surprisingly good at generating text” inside images and is capable of high‑fidelity image creation【Source 6】. In addition, users of the ChatGPT Plus (GPT‑5.x) service discuss daily caps on how many images they can generate – for example, a limit of roughly 180‑200 images per day【Source 8】 and a daily maximum message indicating a cap of 100‑200 images【Source 7】. These discussions confirm that ChatGPT does provide image generation functionality, albeit with usage limits, rather than excluding it altogether.
