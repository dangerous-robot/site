According to "How to ask OpenAI for your personal data to be deleted or not used to train its AIs," EU users can request deletion of personal data from OpenAI through web forms as part of GDPR rights. However, the article suggests this process may be incomplete, noting that OpenAI "retains certain data from your interactions" and takes only partial steps to reduce personal information in training datasets. The source characterizes OpenAI's approach as potentially involving "compliance theatre" and using dark patterns to discourage data deletion requests.
