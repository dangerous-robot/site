OpenAI created a separate AI Text Classifier tool to detect AI-generated content, but ChatGPT itself does not label AI-generated content. According to TechCrunch and The Verge, OpenAI released this external classifier tool in early 2023 with limited accuracy, correctly identifying only 26% of AI-written text as 'likely AI-written.' The tool operates independently of ChatGPT and requires users to input text for analysis, rather than ChatGPT automatically labeling its own outputs or other AI-generated content during conversations.
