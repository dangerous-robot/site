Google’s public posts about Gemini report detailed measurements of the model’s energy consumption, carbon emissions, and water use per text prompt (e.g., median 0.24 Wh, 0.03 gCO₂e) and describe efficiency improvements and data‑center PUE (Source 1: "Measuring the environmental impact of AI inference"; Source 3: "Our approach to energy innovation and AI’s environmental footprint"). However, none of the provided sources specify the electricity generation mix or other details about where the energy powering Gemini originates. The crypto‑exchange Gemini press release (Source 4) concerns a different company and does not address AI energy sourcing. Because there is no evidence that Gemini discloses the source of its energy, the claim that it does so cannot be verified from the given materials.
