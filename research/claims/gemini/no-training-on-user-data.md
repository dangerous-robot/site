Multiple official Google sources consistently state that Gemini does not use user data for model training. According to "How Gemini for Google Cloud uses your data," Gemini doesn't use prompts or responses as training data. The "Generative AI Security, Compliance and Privacy" documentation confirms that data is not used for generative AI model training outside the user's domain without permission. The "Generative AI in Google Workspace Privacy Hub" explicitly states that "Workspace does not use customer data for training models without customer's prior permission or instruction." These commitments apply across Gemini's various implementations including Google Cloud, Google Workspace, and standalone applications.
