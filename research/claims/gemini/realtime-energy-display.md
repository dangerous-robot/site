The claim that Gemini displays real‑time energy usage would require the Gemini interface to show, for each prompt, the exact amount of electricity, water, or carbon emissions being consumed at inference time. The two supplied sources describe how Google measures and publicly reports Gemini’s per‑prompt environmental impact (e.g., 0.24 Wh of energy, 0.26 mL of water, 0.03 g CO₂e) but do not mention any user‑facing feature that displays this data in real time. Source 1 (Google Cloud) outlines measurement methodology and median‑prompt figures, while Source 2 (MIT Technology Review) reports the same numbers and notes Google’s release of the data, not a UI element. Because the sources neither confirm nor deny the existence of a real‑time display within Gemini, the claim cannot be verified based on the available evidence.
