Google states it has maintained "a 100% renewable energy match on a global basis every year since 2017" and has signed over 170 agreements to purchase more than 22 GW of clean energy generation from 2010 to 2024. Since Gemini runs on Google's data center infrastructure, which is covered by this renewable energy commitment, the claim is well-supported. Multiple Google sources confirm their comprehensive renewable energy strategy and ongoing investments in clean energy infrastructure that powers their AI services including Gemini.
