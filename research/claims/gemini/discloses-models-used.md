Google provides comprehensive documentation of its Gemini model offerings through its developer documentation, including detailed specifications for models like Gemini 3.1 Pro and Gemini 2.5 Flash across multiple generations and specialized use cases. However, the Stanford Center for Research on Foundation Models transparency report indicates that Google received low scores on most disclosure indicators, often citing security concerns as reasons for non-disclosure of granular details. TechCrunch reported that experts criticized Google's technical report for Gemini 2.5 Pro as "very sparse" and containing "minimal information," suggesting incomplete transparency despite the availability of basic model specifications.
