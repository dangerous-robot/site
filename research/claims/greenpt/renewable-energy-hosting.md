GreenPT claims its inference runs on 100% renewable energy through Infomaniak's Swiss data centers, which are powered by hydroelectric energy and hold ISO 14001 and ISO 50001 certifications. This claim covers inference-time energy only. The Mistral models GreenPT uses were trained on conventional infrastructure, a tension that applies across the industry. Confidence is medium because the renewable hosting claim relies on Infomaniak's self-reported certifications rather than independent energy auditing of GreenPT specifically.
