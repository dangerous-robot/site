GreenPT's commitment to data protection is further reinforced by its compliance with EU GDPR and ISO-certified data centers (Source 5). The comparison with ChatGPT and Gemini (Sources 4 and 6) highlights its privacy and sustainability advantages without any evidence contrary to this claim. However, without third-party audits or independent validation mechanisms for this specific claim, the best available evidence points to GreenPT's self-stated practices.
