Ecosia publishes comprehensive environmental reporting through multiple channels. According to "Transparency at Ecosia," the company publishes "monthly financial reports and regular updates from our tree-planting projects and partners" as well as "yearly regeneration reports showing tree-planting investments and sustainability metrics." This is evidenced by their 2023 regeneration report "You can't nurse the Earth back to health with half-measures," which details climate action investments, renewable energy projects, and biodiversity outcomes. Additionally, "Ecosia's financial reports" confirms they publish "monthly reports that show exactly how much money we made, and how we use it" for transparency purposes.
