Multiple sources confirm that Ecosia operates under both steward-ownership and B Corporation structures. According to the Purpose Foundation and Steward Ownership Community profiles, Ecosia transitioned to steward-ownership in 2018, implementing a Golden Share model that prevents the company from being sold while ensuring control remains with people directly involved in its mission. Additionally, B Lab Global certifies that Ecosia GmbH has been a Certified B Corporation since April 2014, earning a B Impact Score of 106.9. The steward-ownership model protects Ecosia's environmental mission from commercial sale, while the B Corporation certification demonstrates its commitment to social and environmental performance standards.
