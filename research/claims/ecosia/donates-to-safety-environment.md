Ecosia dedicates 100% of its profits to climate action according to "How Ecosia makes money," with Snopes verifying that 52% of total income (approximately 80% of surplus revenue) goes to tree-planting projects. "Transparency at Ecosia" confirms they focus on avoiding emissions and offsetting unavoidable ones with carbon credits. However, the sources do not provide evidence of donations specifically to AI safety causes, only environmental initiatives.
