Ecosia's AI chat feature uses GPT-4 mini, which runs on OpenAI's infrastructure (Azure data centers). While Ecosia claims 200% renewable energy for its own servers, the AI inference happens on Microsoft/Azure infrastructure where Ecosia has no control over the energy mix. The renewable energy claim covers Ecosia's search servers but does not extend to the AI chat backend. This gap between Ecosia's broad renewable claims and the reality of third-party AI hosting has been noted by critics.
