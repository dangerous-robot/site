According to "Generative AI on Ecosia Search," the company states that "Like all generative AI, Ecosia's AI Search and AI Overviews can: Be factually wrong or outdated ('hallucinate' information)." This demonstrates clear transparency about the potential for AI-generated inaccuracies. The assessment is based primarily on Ecosia's own documentation, with limited independent verification available.
