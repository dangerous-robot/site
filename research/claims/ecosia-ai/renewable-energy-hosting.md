Ecosia explicitly states that they "generate more renewable energy than our AI features use, from 100% clean sources like solar and wind" according to their announcement "Is there a green AI alternative?". The company has invested €18 million in renewable energy projects and built their own solar plants to power their operations, as detailed in "Carbon neutral is not enough". Forbes confirms Ecosia's commitment to being "carbon-negative" rather than just carbon-neutral. However, the evidence relies primarily on Ecosia's own statements and promotional materials.
