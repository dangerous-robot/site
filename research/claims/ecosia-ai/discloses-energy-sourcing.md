Ecosia provides some disclosure about its AI energy sourcing through company communications. In "What we're doing with AI," Ecosia states it "generate[s] more renewable energy than our AI features use, from 100% clean sources like solar and wind." The company's "Transparency at Ecosia" page describes their general approach of "producing twice as much renewable energy as needed for Ecosia searches" and publishing monthly financial reports. However, the disclosures lack technical specificity about AI infrastructure, data center locations, or third-party energy sourcing arrangements that would constitute comprehensive energy disclosure.
