Ecosia provides some information about their AI model choices but lacks comprehensive documentation of selection criteria. According to "Generative AI on Ecosia Search," AI Overviews use Mistral-small-3.2 while AI Chat uses OpenAI's GPT-4.1 mini. "Is there a green AI alternative?" explains their approach uses "smaller, more efficient models" and avoids "energy-heavy features like video generation." However, the sources do not detail the specific technical, environmental, or performance criteria used to evaluate and select these particular models over alternatives.
