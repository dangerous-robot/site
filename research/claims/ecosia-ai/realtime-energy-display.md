Ecosia's own documentation and marketing materials make no mention of displaying real-time energy usage to users. While Ecosia claims to "generate more clean energy from solar and wind than our AI features consume" according to their help documentation, they do not provide actual energy consumption data or real-time usage displays. Climate writer Ketan Joshi specifically criticizes Ecosia for providing "no actual energy consumption data" despite their green AI claims. The available sources consistently show Ecosia makes general claims about renewable energy usage but does not offer transparent, real-time energy monitoring features.
