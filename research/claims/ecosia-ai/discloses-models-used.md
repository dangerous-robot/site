Ecosia provides specific disclosure of the AI models powering its features in its support documentation. According to "Generative AI on Ecosia Search," the company uses Mistral-small-3.2 for AI Overviews and OpenAI GPT-4.1 mini for AI Chat features. This information is made available to users through their official documentation, demonstrating transparency about their model selection.
