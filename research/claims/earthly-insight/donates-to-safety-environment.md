Multiple sources confirm that Earthly Insight commits 33% of its subscription revenue to environmental causes through rewilding projects. According to "Earthly Insight" and "Our Mission," the company channels this revenue to environmental non-profits focused on global rewilding efforts. The partnership is independently verified by "Earthly Insight joins our Global Rewilding Champions," which confirms the company as the Global Rewilding Alliance's first private-sector champion. The sources indicate the company has supported 6+ rewilding projects, demonstrating active implementation of their commitment.
