Earthly Insight positions itself as an AI alternative focused on environmental responsibility rather than direct guidance on AI alternatives. According to "Eco-Friendly AI? How Earthly Insight Is Changing Tech," co-founder Matthew Plotkin describes the service as "A household product alternative" and states they are "not in a race to compete with the clouds, the Googles, the open AIs of the world from a feature standpoint." The company provides AI assistant services using GPT, Gemini, and Claude models according to their company description, but frames itself as an eco-conscious alternative rather than a guidance service about AI options. While it offers an alternative approach to AI services, the sources don't indicate it provides guidance about other AI alternatives.
