Ecosia, the search engine that plants trees, announced its certification as a B Corporation in 2014, demonstrating its commitment to using business practices to address social and environmental challenges. The certification aligns the company with other impact-focused businesses and reinforces its founding principle of prioritizing environmental impact over profits.
