The blog post details Ecosia's diversification from tree planting to broader climate investments. Projects include European venture capital funding, solar installations in Germany and the Global South, regenerative agriculture startups, and biodiversity conservation efforts. The company emphasizes supporting entrepreneurs who are developing solutions for climate action and sustainable food systems.
