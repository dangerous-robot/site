This arXiv paper by Markus Anderljung and 23 co-authors proposes regulatory approaches for "frontier AI" models with potentially dangerous capabilities. The authors outline three building blocks for regulation: standard-setting processes, registration and reporting requirements, and compliance mechanisms.
