  in its data centres
- For a simple conversation of 20-50 questions, the water consumed is equivalent to
  a 500ml bottle

Earth.Org examines the often-overlooked environmental costs of training and operating large language models like ChatGPT. The article details specific carbon emissions and water consumption figures while discussing the lack of transparency from cloud providers and data centers.
