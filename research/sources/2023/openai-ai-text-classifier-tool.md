OpenAI launched the OpenAI AI Text Classifier to address concerns about AI-generated content misuse in education and online platforms. The tool was trained on text from 34 text-generating systems and requires a minimum of 1,000 characters to analyze content. Despite its limitations, the tool represents an early attempt to address the growing challenge of detecting AI-generated text as generative AI becomes more widespread.
