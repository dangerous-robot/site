The Long-Term Benefit Trust (LTBT) is an independent body of five Trustees with expertise in AI safety, national security, public policy, and social enterprise. The Trust holds Class T stock that grants it authority to elect board members according to time and funding-based milestones, ultimately securing a majority of the board within four years. The structure is paired with Anthropic's Public Benefit Corporation status to address governance challenges posed by transformative AI's large externalities and long-term societal consequences.
