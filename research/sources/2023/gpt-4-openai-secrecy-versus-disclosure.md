- Given both the competitive landscape and the safety implications of large-scale
  models like GPT-4, This report contains no further details about the architecture
  (including model size), hardware, training compute, dataset construction, training
  method, or similar.
- Without such details, the GPT-4 program is a complete enigma. The research paper
  in that sense discloses none of the research.
- Due to our concerns about malicious applications of the technology, we are not releasing
  the trained model

The article examines how OpenAI's GPT-4 technical report marked a departure from traditional AI research transparency. Unlike previous GPT releases which included architectural details, GPT-4's paper intentionally withheld technical specifications citing competitive and safety concerns.
