MIT Technology Review's investigation measures AI's energy demands per query, finding that a single ChatGPT message uses roughly 1,080 joules—comparable to riding six feet on an e-bike. The analysis reveals that data centers supporting AI consumed 53-76 terawatt-hours in 2024 and projects this could rise to 165-326 terawatt-hours by 2028, representing a fundamental shift in US electricity demand driven primarily by AI infrastructure expansion.
