Anthropic announced Claude Opus 4.5 on November 24, 2025, positioning it as the company's most capable general-purpose model. The announcement includes benchmarks showing superior performance on software engineering (SWE-bench Multilingual), long-horizon autonomous tasks (Terminal Bench), and agentic capabilities (BrowseComp-Plus). New developer platform features include an "effort parameter" for controlling reasoning depth and token usage, context management for multi-agent systems, and product integrations in Claude Code, Chrome, and Excel.
