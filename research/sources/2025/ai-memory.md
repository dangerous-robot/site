The article discusses the 'Memory Paradox,' where reliance on digital tools for cognitive offloading may erode essential memory skills and intuitive reasoning. It suggests that educational frameworks should reintegrate retrieval practice and spaced repetition to maintain human cognitive capabilities alongside AI.
