This blog post from Deloitte's Pulse Blog provides detailed guidance on implementing AI governance frameworks for finance and accounting functions. It includes a practical case study of invoice classification using computer vision and large language models, demonstrating specific testing metrics and control procedures. The content is based on a presentation given at the Financial Executives International AI conference.
