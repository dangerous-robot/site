Climate analyst Ketan Joshi examines Ecosia's recent pivot to AI-powered search summaries, arguing that the not-for-profit search engine is abandoning its environmental principles by implementing energy-intensive AI features while making unsubstantiated "green AI" claims. He demonstrates how the AI system plagiarizes and distorts content from creators, using his own work as examples of fabricated quotes and misattributed content.
