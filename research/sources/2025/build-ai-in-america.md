Anthropic released this policy report in July 2025 outlining infrastructure and regulatory requirements for developing frontier AI models in the United States. The report proposes two strategic pillars: large-scale training infrastructure development and broad-based nationwide deployment support, including recommendations for accelerating permitting, grid interconnection, and workforce development. It emphasizes the urgency of energy infrastructure expansion relative to competing nations, particularly China's rapid power capacity additions.
