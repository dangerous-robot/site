  can help set a baseline for how responsible labs train their models.

Anthropic outlines a framework for AI transparency policy that would apply only to the largest frontier model developers, requiring them to publish Secure Development Frameworks and system cards while protecting against regulatory overreach. The proposal aims to standardize voluntary industry safety practices into legally required disclosures.
