Microsoft's carbon credit strategy involves a two-pronged approach: aggressively reducing its own emissions and offsetting the rest with high-quality carbon removals. The company has invested in various innovative carbon removal technologies, such as biochar, afforestation, and bioenergy with carbon capture and storage (BECCS).
