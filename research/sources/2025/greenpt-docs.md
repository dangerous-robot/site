GreenPT's documentation describes their hosting arrangement with Infomaniak on Swiss renewable energy infrastructure. The service uses open-weight models (Mistral Small, Mistral Large) and provides per-call energy measurements in Watt-milliseconds and emissions in micrograms CO2e through its API. Data jurisdiction falls under Swiss FADP and GDPR.
