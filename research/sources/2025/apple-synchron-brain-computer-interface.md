Apple has integrated brain-computer interface support into its major operating systems through the Switch Control feature, partnering with Synchron to test the technology on patients with severe disabilities. The Stentrode implant is inserted through blood vessels rather than requiring surgical drilling into the skull, representing a less invasive approach compared to competitors like Neuralink.
