Climate and tech analyst Ketan Joshi critiques the not-for-profit search engine Ecosia's recent implementation of AI-generated search summaries, arguing it contradicts their environmental mission. The piece examines Ecosia's \"world's greenest AI\" claims while highlighting the lack of transparent energy usage data and the broader trend of \"hedonistic sustainability\" in corporate climate initiatives.
