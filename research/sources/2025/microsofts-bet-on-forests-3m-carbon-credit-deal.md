Microsoft's deal with EFM includes purchasing up to 3 million carbon removal credits and investing in sustainable forestry projects. This initiative supports Microsoft's goal of becoming carbon negative by 2030 and includes efforts to remove all carbon emitted since the company's founding by 2050.
