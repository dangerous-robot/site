summary: Anthropic will start using Claude chat logs for training its models; users
  can opt out via privacy settings, with data retention up to five years for opted‑in
  users.
key_quotes:
- “All large language models, like Claude, are trained using large amounts of data,”
- “Allow the use of your chats and coding sessions to train and improve Anthropic
  AI models,”
- The toggle to provide your data to Anthropic to train Claude is automatically on,
  so users who chose to accept the updates without clicking that toggle are opted
  into the new training policy.
- Anthropic increased the amount of time it holds onto user data from 30 days in most
  situations to a much more extensive five years.
- Commercial users, like those licensed through government or educational plans, are
  not impacted by the change and conversations from those users will not be used as
  part of the company’s model training.
