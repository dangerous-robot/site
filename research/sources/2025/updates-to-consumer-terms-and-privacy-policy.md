  and strengthen our safeguards against harmful usage like scams and abuse.
- If you delete a conversation with Claude it will not be used for future model training.
- We are also extending data retention to five years, if you allow us to use your
  data for model training.
- You have until October 8, 2025 to make your selection.
- We do not sell users’ data to third parties.

The announcement details changes to Anthropic’s consumer agreements, introducing an opt‑in for using user data to train future Claude models and extending retention from 30 days to five years. The updates apply to Claude Free, Pro, and Max plans but not to commercial or API services.
