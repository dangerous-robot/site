This post announces the Future of Life Institute's Summer 2025 AI Safety Index, evaluating seven leading AI companies using ratings from six independent experts. The report reveals that most firms are unprepared to manage risks associated with their own advancements, particularly concerning dangerous capabilities like bio- or cyber-terrorism. Notably, only three firms reported substantive testing in these areas. The exercise also highlights gaps in whistleblowing policies and varying levels of transparency.
