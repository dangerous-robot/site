The paper presents a large‑scale online survey of 319 knowledge workers who shared 936 real‑world examples of GenAI use at work. Quantitative mixed‑effects regression models examine how task‑specific and user‑level factors relate to perceived enaction and effort of critical thinking. The authors discuss motivators, inhibitors, and design implications for supporting critical thinking in AI‑assisted knowledge work.
