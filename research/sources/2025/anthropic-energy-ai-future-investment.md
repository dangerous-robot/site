Anthropic CEO Dario Amodei announced this investment at the Pennsylvania Energy and Innovation Summit alongside President Trump and Senator Dave McCormick. The funding supports the Scott Institute for Energy Innovation and the picoCTF cybersecurity education program, reflecting Anthropic's broader strategy to strengthen America's AI infrastructure and energy independence.
