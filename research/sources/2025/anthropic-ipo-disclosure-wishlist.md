The authors, including Tim O'Reilly and researchers from the AI Disclosures Project, outline specific SEC disclosure requirements that would apply to Anthropic's potential IPO. They argue for transparency around circular revenue deals with cloud providers, training data litigation costs including the $1.5 billion settlement with authors, and crucially, what metrics and objectives Anthropic's algorithms optimize for during fine-tuning and reinforcement learning.
