Anthropic CEO Dario Amodei presented these priorities at the inaugural Pennsylvania Energy and Innovation Summit at Carnegie Mellon University in July 2025. The $2 million contribution is split equally: $1 million for the Scott Institute for Energy Innovation to develop AI-powered grid management solutions, and $1 million for the picoCTF cybersecurity education program for middle and high school students. Anthropic frames energy infrastructure investment as essential to maintaining U.S. competitiveness in AI development and deployment.
