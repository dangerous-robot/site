Microsoft has signed a significant deal with Rubicon Carbon to purchase 18 million tonnes of carbon removal credits over the next 15 to 20 years. This agreement is one of the largest of its kind and highlights the role of major corporations in scaling climate solutions. The carbon credits will come from Afforestation, Reforestation, and Revegetation (ARR) projects worldwide, managed by Rubicon Carbon to ensure high scientific standards.
