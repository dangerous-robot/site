Anthropic released this policy report in July 2025 outlining infrastructure requirements for maintaining U.S. AI leadership. The report proposes two strategic pillars: accelerating large-scale AI training infrastructure through federal land access and NEPA expediting, and building broad-based nationwide infrastructure through geothermal, natural gas, and nuclear permitting reforms. Key projections indicate the U.S. AI sector requires 50GW of electricity capacity by 2028, roughly double New York City's peak demand.
