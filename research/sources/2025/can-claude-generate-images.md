Data Studios published this article in May 2025 explaining that Claude lacks image generation capabilities despite its powerful vision features. The piece contrasts Claude's ability to analyze images with tools like DALL-E 3, Midjourney, and Stable Diffusion that create images from scratch, and provides practical workflows for using Claude to draft and critique prompts for external image generators.
