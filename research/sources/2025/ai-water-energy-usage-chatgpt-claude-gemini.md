This report provides detailed benchmarking data on energy consumption across 30+ AI models, comparing inference costs, training impacts, and water usage. It includes case studies of the major hyperscalers (Microsoft, Google, AWS, Meta) and their sustainability strategies, as well as comparative analysis of AI's environmental footprint relative to other digital activities like video streaming and web searches.
