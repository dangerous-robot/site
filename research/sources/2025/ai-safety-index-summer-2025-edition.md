This report, produced by Future of Life Institute (FLI) and AI Governance Library (AIGL), assesses the safety practices of leading AI developers like Anthropic, OpenAI, and Google DeepMind. It emphasizes a lack of robust measures, especially around existential risks, and highlights methodology built on publicly verifiable evidence.
