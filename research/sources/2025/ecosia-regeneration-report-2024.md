Ecosia's annual regeneration report details their environmental achievements for 2024, including tree planting across new countries like Togo, Vietnam, and the Netherlands. The company expanded beyond reforestation to include biodiversity conservation through the Global Biodiversity Standard and ecosystem restoration projects like peatland re-wetting.
