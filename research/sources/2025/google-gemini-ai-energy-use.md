  about one second.
- In this measurement, the AI chips—in this case, Google’s custom TPUs, the company’s

  of 0.24 watt-hours.
- AI data centers also consume water for cooling, and Google estimates that each prompt

- Google also estimates the greenhouse-gas emissions associated with the median prompt,
  which they put at 0.03 grams of carbon dioxide.

The article covers Google’s newly published technical report on Gemini’s energy, water and carbon footprints for text prompts, including hardware‑level breakdowns and commentary from Jeff Dean. It notes the median prompt uses 0.24 Wh, 0.26 mL water, and emits 0.03 g CO₂, with AI chips responsible for 58% of the electricity demand.
