Anthropic announced a policy change allowing user chat transcripts to be used for Claude model training, with a September 28, 2025 opt-out deadline for existing users. Data retention would extend to five years for users who opt in, though users can disable this in settings. Anthropic claims the change will improve model capabilities and safety measures.
