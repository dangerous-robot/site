Anthropic announced policy changes on August 28, 2025, allowing Free, Pro, and Max plan users to voluntarily share conversation data for model training and safety improvements. The update extends data retention from 30 days to 5 years for users who opt in, with a transition deadline of October 8, 2025. The policy explicitly excludes commercial services including Claude for Work, API access, and government/education offerings.
