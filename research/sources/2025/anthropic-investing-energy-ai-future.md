Anthropic CEO Dario Amodei announced the company's $2 million contribution to Carnegie Mellon University at the Pennsylvania Energy and Innovation Summit, with funds split equally between the Scott Institute for Energy Innovation ($1M for AI-driven grid optimization) and the picoCTF cybersecurity education program ($1M for middle and high school student training). The company frames energy infrastructure and workforce development as essential to American AI competitiveness and emphasizes AI's potential role in emissions reduction and clean energy innovation.
