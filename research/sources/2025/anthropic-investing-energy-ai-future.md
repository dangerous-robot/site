Anthropic's CEO Dario Amodei spoke at Carnegie Mellon University's inaugural Pennsylvania Energy and Innovation Summit alongside President Trump and Senator Dave McCormick. The funding supports the Scott Institute for Energy Innovation's grid management research and the picoCTF cybersecurity education program for students.
