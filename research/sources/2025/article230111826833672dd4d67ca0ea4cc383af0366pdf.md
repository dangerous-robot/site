- 'Outcomes were predominantly positive: multiple studies improved immediate retention
  and demonstrated advantages at delay'

  mnemonics), targeted metacognitive or affective processes, or personalized prompts

The review synthesizes research across K-12, higher education, and adult learning, comparing AI-enabled tools against traditional instructional methods. It maps findings to cognitive frameworks such as Cognitive Load Theory and the testing effect.
