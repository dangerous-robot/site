The FLI AI Safety Index evaluates major AI companies on risk assessment, current harms, safety frameworks, existential safety, governance and accountability, and information sharing. Anthropic and OpenAI tied for the highest overall grade at C+, followed by Google DeepMind at C. The remaining companies (xAI, Z.ai, Meta, DeepSeek, Alibaba Cloud) scored D or D-.
