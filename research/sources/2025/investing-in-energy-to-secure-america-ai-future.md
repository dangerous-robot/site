Anthropic CEO Dario Amodei announced this initiative at the Pennsylvania Energy and Innovation Summit at Carnegie Mellon University on July 15, 2025. The contribution was split equally between the Scott Institute for Energy Innovation ($1 million) to advance AI-driven grid management and optimization, and the picoCTF program ($1 million) to provide cybersecurity education to middle and high school students. Anthropic positions energy infrastructure and cybersecurity as critical to maintaining American leadership in AI development and deployment.
