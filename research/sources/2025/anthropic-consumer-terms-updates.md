Anthropic announced on August 28, 2025 updates to its Consumer Terms and Privacy Policy for Claude Free, Pro, and Max users. The changes enable users to opt-in to share their conversations and coding sessions for model training and improvement of safety classifiers. Users receive until October 8, 2025 to make their selection, with an extended five-year data retention period for those who opt-in, versus the existing 30-day retention for non-participants.
