The blog post examines the introduction of Ecosia Chat, an OpenAI-powered chatbot integrated into the eco-friendly search engine, and the resulting user backlash over accuracy, privacy, and control concerns. It provides detailed analysis of AI's environmental impact, showing chatbots use 10x more electricity and produce 300x higher CO₂ emissions than traditional search queries.
