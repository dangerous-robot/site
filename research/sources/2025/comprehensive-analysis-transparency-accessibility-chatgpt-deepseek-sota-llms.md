An academic paper authored by Ranjan Sapkota, Shaina Raza, and Manoj Karkee that systematically examines transparency issues in modern large language models. The study addresses concerns about "open-washing" where AI models claim openness but lack full transparency, limiting reproducibility and bias mitigation efforts.
