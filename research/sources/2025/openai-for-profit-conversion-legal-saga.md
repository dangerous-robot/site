The article reports on OpenAI's completion of its corporate restructuring from nonprofit to public benefit corporation, approved by Delaware's attorney general after over a year of negotiations. The conversion gives Microsoft a 27% stake worth over $100bn while maintaining the nonprofit's technical control over the for-profit entity.
