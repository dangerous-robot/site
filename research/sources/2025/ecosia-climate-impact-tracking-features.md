The green search engine introduces an impact counter system where users collect seeds daily and can track their contributions to climate initiatives including renewable energy, regenerative agriculture, and biodiversity protection. CEO Christian Kroll emphasizes this addresses the gap between Ecosia's broader climate investments beyond tree planting and user awareness of their collective impact.
