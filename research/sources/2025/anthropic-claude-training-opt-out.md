WIRED's Reece Rogers explains Anthropic's shift from not training models on user conversations to a default opt-in policy that took effect October 8, 2025. The article details the opt-out process via Privacy Settings and notes that data retention for participating users extended from 30 days to five years. Commercial and government-licensed users are exempt from the training policy changes.
