The article examines criticism from AI safety experts regarding Google's technical report for its Gemini 2.5 Pro model, which they argue lacks sufficient safety evaluation details. The report was published weeks after the model's launch and omits key information about dangerous capability assessments. Experts suggest this represents a broader trend of reduced transparency in AI safety reporting across major tech companies.
