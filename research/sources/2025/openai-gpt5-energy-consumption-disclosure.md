University of Rhode Island researchers found GPT-5 uses an average of 18 watt-hours for medium-length responses, with OpenAI providing no official energy consumption data since GPT-3. The model's enhanced capabilities including reasoning, video, and image processing contribute to significantly higher energy demands compared to text-only predecessors.
