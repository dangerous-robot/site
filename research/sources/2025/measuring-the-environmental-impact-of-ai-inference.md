  emits 0.03 grams of carbon dioxide equivalent (gCO2e), and consumes 0.26 milliliters

- the energy and total carbon footprint of the median Gemini Apps text prompt dropped
  by 33x and 44x, respectively

The post outlines Google's 'full-stack' approach to AI efficiency, including custom TPU hardware, Mixture-of-Experts (MoE) architectures, and data center optimizations. It argues that comprehensive measurement must include idle machines and data center overhead to avoid underestimating the operational footprint.
