- We estimate the median Gemini Apps text prompt uses 0.24 watt‑hours (Wh) of energy,
  emits 0.03 grams of carbon dioxide equivalent (gCO2e), and consumes 0.26 milliliters
  (or about five drops) of water.
- The per‑prompt energy impact is equivalent to watching TV for less than nine seconds.
- Google’s data centers are among the industry’s most efficient, operating at a fleet‑wide
  average PUE of 1.09.
- Over a recent 12‑month period, the energy and total carbon footprint of the median
  Gemini Apps text prompt dropped by 33× and 44×, respectively.
- Our latest‑generation TPU, Ironwood, is 30× more energy‑efficient than our first
  publicly‑available TPU.
