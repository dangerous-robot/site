This report documents Anthropic's responses to 35 indicators covering transparency in foundation model training, development, and deployment—part of Stanford's CRFM Foundation Model Transparency Index (FMTI) December 2025 assessment. The evaluation covers data sourcing, laborer practices, computational infrastructure, model architecture, and organizational structure, with most indicators receiving a score of 0 due to proprietary disclosure restrictions.
