- AI tools offer significant potential to improve nonprofit workflows, but like any
  technology, they come with tradeoffs.
- In 2023, data centers accounted for roughly 4.4 percent of all electricity used
  in the U.S.
- AI currently only makes up an estimated 14 percent of data center workloads.
- The AI activities most common for nonprofits, such as summarizing notes, drafting
  an email, or brainstorming fundraising ideas, represent an even tinier fraction
  of that footprint.

This article addresses nonprofit concerns about AI's environmental impact, offering practical guidance for responsible technology adoption. It contextualizes AI's relatively small footprint within overall data center usage while providing actionable steps for minimizing environmental impact.
