- The median Gemini Apps text prompt uses 0.24 watt-hours (Wh) of energy, emits 0.03
  grams of carbon dioxide equivalent (gCO₂e), and consumes 0.26 milliliters (or about
  five drops) of water.
- The per‑prompt energy impact is equivalent to watching TV for less than nine seconds.
- Over a recent 12 month period, the energy and total carbon footprint of the median
  Gemini Apps text prompt dropped by 33x and 44x, respectively.
- Google’s data centers are among the industry’s most efficient, operating at a fleet‑wide
  average PUE of 1.09.
- We continue to add clean energy generation in pursuit of our 24/7 carbon‑free ambition,
  while advancing our aim to replenish 120% of the freshwater we consume on average
  across our offices and data centers.
