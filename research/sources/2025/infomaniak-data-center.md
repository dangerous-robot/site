Infomaniak's Geneva data center recovers 100% of its waste heat to feed the Geneva district heating network. The facility operates with a Power Usage Effectiveness (PUE) of 1.06, compared to the European average of approximately 1.8. It uses no water cooling and runs on Swiss hydroelectric power. The data center holds ISO 14001 and ISO 50001 certifications, audited annually by SGS.
