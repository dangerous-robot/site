Anthropic announced Claude for Nonprofits in December 2025 in partnership with GivingTuesday. The program provides up to 75% discounts on Team and Enterprise plans, integration with nonprofit-focused tools, and free educational resources. The announcement includes case studies from organizations like the Epilepsy Foundation, International Rescue Committee, and IDinsight demonstrating AI's impact on their operations.
