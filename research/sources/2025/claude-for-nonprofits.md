Anthropic's Claude for Nonprofits initiative, announced in partnership with GivingTuesday, provides discounted access to Claude Team and Enterprise plans at up to 75% off, along with new integrations for Benevity, Blackbaud, and Candid platforms. The program includes a free "AI Fluency for Nonprofits" course and step-by-step guides for nonprofit workflows such as grant writing and impact reporting. Several nonprofit partners including the Epilepsy Foundation, International Rescue Committee, and IDinsight demonstrate early adoption of Claude for various mission-critical tasks including 24/7 support, humanitarian coordination, and data analysis.
