Google's 10th annual Environmental Report highlights progress in decoupling energy growth from operational carbon emissions through 25 clean energy projects adding 2.5 gigawatts of capacity in 2024. The report acknowledges increased total emissions from supply chain activities and grid limitations in certain regions while showcasing AI applications that helped reduce 26 million metric tons of CO2 equivalent emissions across five products. Future sustainability efforts focus on next-generation clean technologies including advanced nuclear and enhanced geothermal systems.
