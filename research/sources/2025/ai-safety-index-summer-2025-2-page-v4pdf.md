The two‑page index provides an executive summary, methodology, and a list of the seven evaluated companies (Anthropic, OpenAI, DeepMind, x.AI, Meta, Zhipu AI, Deepseek) and their grades across 34 safety indicators. It is authored by an independent panel of AI safety experts.
