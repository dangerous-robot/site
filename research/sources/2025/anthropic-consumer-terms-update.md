Anthropic announced on August 28, 2025, updates to its consumer terms for Claude Free, Pro, and Max users, giving them the option to allow their chat and coding data to be used for model improvement and safety enhancement. The changes include an extended five-year data retention period for users who opt-in, while those who decline maintain a 30-day retention policy. These updates do not apply to commercial services including Claude for Work, Claude for Government, Claude for Education, or API usage.
