Anthropic announced updates to consumer terms and privacy policies on August 28, 2025, allowing Claude Free, Pro, and Max plan users to opt-in to data sharing for model training and safety improvements. Users opting in receive five-year data retention instead of the standard 30 days, with full control available in Privacy Settings at any time. The policy excludes commercial services including Claude for Work, API access, and government/education offerings.
