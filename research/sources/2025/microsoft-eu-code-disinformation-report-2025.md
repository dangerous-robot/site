The report details Microsoft's cross-product approach to Responsible AI, its participation in the Tech Accord to Combat Deceptive Use of AI, and specific integrity measures for Bing, LinkedIn, and Microsoft Advertising. It covers the reporting period from July 1, 2024, to December 31, 2024.
