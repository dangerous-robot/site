This report is part of Stanford's Foundation Model Transparency Initiative (FMTI) December 2025 evaluation, systematically assessing Anthropic's public disclosures against 35 transparency indicators covering data, compute, and model development practices. The assessment reveals that Anthropic disclosed limited information across most indicators, citing proprietary concerns and competitive advantage protection, while providing some detail on data acquisition methods, crawler practices, and user notification mechanisms for data usage.
