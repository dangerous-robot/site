- we estimate the median Gemini Apps text prompt uses 0.24 watt-hours (Wh) of energy,
  emits 0.03 grams of carbon dioxide equivalent (gCO2e), and consumes 0.26 milliliters
  (or about five drops) of water
- over a recent 12 month period, the energy and total carbon footprint of the median
  Gemini Apps text prompt dropped by 33x and 44x, respectively
- The per-prompt energy impact is equivalent to watching TV for less than nine seconds
- Many current AI energy consumption calculations only include active machine consumption,
  overlooking several of the critical factors discussed above

Google researchers present a comprehensive methodology for measuring the true environmental cost of AI inference, accounting for factors like idle machines, data center overhead, and cooling systems. The study reveals substantial efficiency improvements in Gemini models over the past year through hardware optimization and architectural advances.
