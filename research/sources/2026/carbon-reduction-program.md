Microsoft's Carbon Reduction Program outlines their commitment to reducing carbon emissions and achieving carbon negativity by 2030. The program focuses on increasing efficiency, building markets, forging partnerships, improving measurement, and advocating for policy. Microsoft aims to reduce Scope 1 and 2 emissions to near zero and Scope 3 emissions by more than half by 2030.
