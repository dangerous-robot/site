The program offers a $3,850 weekly stipend, compute funding (~$15k/month), and mentorship from Anthropic researchers. Research areas include scalable oversight, adversarial robustness, mechanistic interpretability, and model organisms. Previous fellows worked on projects ranging from blockchain vulnerability detection to studying agentic misalignment in simulated corporate environments.
