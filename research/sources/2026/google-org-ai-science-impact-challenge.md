Google.org's Impact Challenge offers $30M in funding for scientific research projects leveraging AI across two focus areas: health & life sciences and climate resilience. Applicants must demonstrate scientific ambition with quantifiable metrics, use AI as a core solution component aligned with responsible AI principles, provide realistic execution plans, and show potential for scaled impact. The program includes optional participation in a Google.org Accelerator providing six months of pro bono technical support and Google Cloud credits.
