ChatGPTree combines AI productivity with environmental action by automatically planting certified trees monthly based on subscription levels. The service partners with Evertreen and Veritree to plant native species that maximize biodiversity and carbon capture, claiming to have planted 3,950 trees and offset 27.1 million chats per year.
