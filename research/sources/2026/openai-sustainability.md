This article from The Sustainable Innovation provides a detailed sustainability assessment of OpenAI as of March 2026. It documents OpenAI's energy consumption, environmental commitments, and significant reporting gaps, with particular emphasis on the absence of published Scope 1, 2, or 3 emissions data despite OpenAI's 2030 carbon neutrality goal. The article tracks OpenAI's Stargate infrastructure program (targeting 10 GW capacity by 2029) and compares OpenAI's environmental accountability to competitors including Microsoft, Google, and Meta.
