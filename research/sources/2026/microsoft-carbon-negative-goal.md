Microsoft's carbon negative commitment is part of a long history of ambitious targets and actions to reduce greenhouse gas emissions. The company has been carbon neutral since 2012 and is now focusing on becoming carbon negative by 2030, including all Scope 1, 2, and 3 emissions. By 2050, Microsoft aims to remove all the carbon it has emitted since its founding in 1975.
