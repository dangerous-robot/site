Anthropic's RSP provides a structured approach to AI safety through defined capability thresholds and corresponding safeguards. Version 3.1, effective April 2, 2026, clarifies the AI R&D capability threshold definition and reaffirms the organization's ability to pause development when appropriate. The page documents RSP evolution from September 2023 to present, including the comprehensive rewrite in Version 3.0 (February 2026) and records instances of partial noncompliance with policy requirements.
