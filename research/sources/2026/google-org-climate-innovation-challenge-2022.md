The challenge focused on funding projects that use AI, satellite data, and geospatial technology to address climate issues like mangrove ecosystem monitoring, urban heat mapping, Arctic permafrost tracking, home electrification modeling, multi-hazard risk assessment, and waste methane detection. Selected organizations include World Wildlife Fund, World Resources Institute, Woodwell Climate Research Center, Rewiring America, The New School, and Global Methane Hub.
