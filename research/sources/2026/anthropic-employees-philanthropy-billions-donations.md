The article examines how the anticipated wealth transfer from Anthropic's $380 billion valuation and upcoming IPO could shape philanthropic priorities in AI safety and effective altruism. It documents existing connections between Anthropic leadership and EA organizations like Open Philanthropy (now Coefficient Giving), while discussing potential conflicts of interest and concerns about funding organizations tasked with monitoring AI company safety practices. The piece also explores where employee donations might flow—from AI safety nonprofits to political campaigns to global health causes.
