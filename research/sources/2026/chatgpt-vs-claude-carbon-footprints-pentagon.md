The article examines the environmental impact of two major AI systems amid geopolitical controversy over OpenAI's Pentagon partnership. It provides quantified comparisons of per-query energy consumption, modeling the scaling effects of billions of daily queries, and discusses implications for data center electricity demand and carbon accounting in climate policy.
