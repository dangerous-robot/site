This page describes Google's research funding program for academics working on AI applications in privacy, safety, and security. The program accepts proposals from university professors across disciplines including computer science, law, policy, and social sciences, and provides unrestricted gifts to support approximately one year of research work. Applications are currently closed, with information on future cycles to be announced.
