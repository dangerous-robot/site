Anthropic released Claude Opus 4.6 on February 5, 2026, as an upgrade featuring enhanced agentic capabilities, extended context handling, and improved performance on complex multi-step tasks. The model introduces a 1M token context window in beta and adaptive thinking features. Early partners reported significant improvements in coding tasks, long-running agentic workflows, and reasoning-intensive work across legal, financial, and cybersecurity domains.
