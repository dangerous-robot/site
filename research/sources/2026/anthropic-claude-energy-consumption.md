This article provides an empirical analysis of Anthropic's Claude AI energy consumption using published inference benchmarks and user-base estimates. The authors acknowledge significant uncertainties around actual prompt volumes and data centre cooling methods, emphasizing that their figures represent order-of-magnitude indicators rather than audited operational data. The analysis highlights a key gap: Anthropic does not publicly disclose energy consumption metrics, forcing researchers to rely on third-party estimates and generic data-centre efficiency metrics.
