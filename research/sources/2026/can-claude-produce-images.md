This is official documentation from Anthropic's Claude Help Center clarifying Claude's image capabilities. The article explains that while Claude cannot generate photos or illustrations like dedicated image-generation tools, it can create interactive diagrams and charts in SVG/HTML format. It also notes that Claude can analyze images uploaded by users.
