Anthropic announced this feature in March 2026, allowing Claude to generate interactive visuals—such as periodic tables and building diagrams—automatically based on conversation context. The visualizations appear directly in-line within the chat, unlike the persistent "artifacts" feature that opens in a side panel. Claude's new capability comes amid similar developments from competitors, including OpenAI's visualization features in ChatGPT and Google Gemini's educational image generation.
