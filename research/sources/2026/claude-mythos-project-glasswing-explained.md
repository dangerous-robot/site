This article from MindStudio provides a comprehensive explanation of Claude Mythos and Project Glasswing, detailing Anthropic's approach to handling frontier models that cross safety thresholds. It describes the Responsible Scaling Policy (RSP) framework with its AI Safety Levels (ASL), the specific capabilities that likely distinguish Mythos, and how Project Glasswing operates through controlled evaluation, red-teaming, and capability elicitation. The piece contextualizes this against how other labs like OpenAI and Meta approach frontier model safety.
