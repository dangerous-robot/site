This documentation provides seven steps for deploying Viro, an AI assistant app that routes tasks between multiple language models (ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, and Qwen) while claiming to offset energy usage through clean energy funding. The guide covers team setup, app import, permissions configuration, and deployment through Microsoft Teams Admin Center.
