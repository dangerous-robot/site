Viro is an AI application developed by a solo founder that addresses the environmental impact of AI usage by funding renewable energy projects. The app supports multiple AI models including ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, and LLaMA, and has achieved over 950 users with 5,000+ kWh of clean energy funded through partnerships with Terrapass.
