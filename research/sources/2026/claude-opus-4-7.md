Claude Opus 4.7 launched on April 16, 2026, representing a significant upgrade from Opus 4.6 with enhanced software engineering performance, improved multimodal vision capabilities, and stronger instruction-following. The model is available across all Claude products, the Claude API, Amazon Bedrock, Google Cloud Vertex AI, and Microsoft Foundry at the same pricing as Opus 4.6. A new "xhigh" effort level was introduced to provide finer control over reasoning-latency tradeoffs, and Anthropic implemented cybersecurity safeguards to restrict high-risk capabilities while launching a Cyber Verification Program for legitimate security research.
