This documentation page from Anthropic's Privacy Center explains data usage policies for Claude consumer products (Free, Pro, Max). It distinguishes between consumer products and commercial offerings (Claude for Work, API), detailing when user chats and coding sessions may be used for model improvement, including exceptions for incognito mode and de-linked feedback procedures.
