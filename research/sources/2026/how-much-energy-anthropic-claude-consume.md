The article estimates Claude AI's energy consumption at approximately 12.76 megawatt-hours per day based on a published benchmark of 4.05 watt-hours per 400-token request and an assumed user base of 18.9 million monthly active users generating five prompts daily. Water consumption is estimated at roughly 24,239 liters per day using standard data-centre water-usage effectiveness metrics. The authors emphasize these are order-of-magnitude indicators rather than official figures, as Anthropic does not publicly disclose definitive energy or water metrics per request.
