This report represents Anthropic's formal safety documentation for Claude models, last updated February 20, 2026. It includes detailed system cards, RSP (Responsible Scaling Policy) evaluations, and safety assessments across CBRN, autonomous AI R&D, and cybersecurity domains. The report covers deployment standards (ASL-2 through ASL-3) and discusses emerging risks including evaluation awareness in newer models and increased cybersecurity capabilities.
