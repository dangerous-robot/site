This paper introduces Constitutional AI, Anthropic's approach to training AI systems that can follow helpful behaviors while avoiding harmful ones. The method combines human oversight with AI-generated feedback based on a written constitution of principles. The research demonstrates how AI systems can be trained to critique and revise their own outputs according to constitutional guidelines.
