Microsoft has achieved a major sustainability milestone by matching 100% of its global electricity use with renewable energy. The company’s renewable energy portfolio includes solar, wind, hydro, and other renewables, with contracts spanning the United States, Europe, the Asia Pacific, and Latin America.
