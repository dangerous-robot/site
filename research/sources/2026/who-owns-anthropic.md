This March 2026 article provides an in-depth analysis of Anthropic's private ownership structure, detailing how the company maintains independence despite receiving billions in investment from Amazon and Google. The article explains the Long-Term Benefit Trust governance mechanism intended to prevent mission drift and analyzes the regulatory scrutiny surrounding large tech investments in AI companies. As of early 2026, Anthropic remained private with a $60+ billion valuation and approximately $2 billion in annualized revenue.
