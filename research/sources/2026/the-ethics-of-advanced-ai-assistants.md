summary: DeepMind discusses the potential benefits and risks of increasingly capable
  AI assistants, covering alignment, safety, misuse, societal impact, and the need
  for foresight and coordinated evaluation.
key_quotes:
- Advanced AI assistants could have a profound impact on users and society, and be
  integrated into most aspects of people’s lives.
- AI assistants will likely have a significant level of autonomy for planning and
  performing sequences of tasks across a range of domains.
- With more autonomy comes greater risk of accidents caused by unclear or misinterpreted
  instructions, and greater risk of assistants taking actions that are misaligned
  with the user’s values and interests.
- Able to fluidly communicate using natural language, the written output and voices
  of advanced AI assistants may become hard to distinguish from those of humans.
- If this technology becomes widely available and deployed at scale, advanced AI assistants
  will need to interact with each other, with users and non‑users alike.
