Anthropic announced in February 2026 that it is contributing $20 million to Public First Action, a new bipartisan 501(c)(4) organization dedicated to public education and advocacy around AI governance. The organization will work across party lines to promote policies including AI model transparency, robust federal governance frameworks, smart export controls on AI chips, and targeted regulation on high-risk AI applications like biological weapons and cyberattacks. Anthropic frames the donation as part of its commitment to ensuring AI technology serves the public good while responsibly managing associated risks.
