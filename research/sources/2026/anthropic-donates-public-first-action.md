Anthropic details its policy priorities including transparency safeguards, federal AI governance frameworks, export controls on AI chips, and targeted regulation for high-risk applications like AI-enabled biological weapons and cyberattacks. The company emphasizes that effective AI governance means more scrutiny of AI developers, not less.
