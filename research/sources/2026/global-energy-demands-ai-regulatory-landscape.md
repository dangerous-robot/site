This comprehensive briefing was prepared for the Forum for Cooperation on AI dialogue and examines how hyperscaler expansion is straining electricity grids and water resources globally. It surveys international governance frameworks including OECD principles, G7 Hiroshima AI Process, UNESCO recommendations, and ISO/IEC standards, while analyzing regional impacts in mature markets (Ireland, Northern Virginia) and emerging AI hubs (Southeast Asia, China). The document discusses both supply-side measures (renewable energy procurement, nuclear power) and demand-side optimization strategies, while highlighting the grid interconnection bottleneck that could delay nearly 20% of planned data center projects.
