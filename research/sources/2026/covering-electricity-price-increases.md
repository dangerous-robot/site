Anthropic announced a policy to address the electricity cost impacts of its growing infrastructure needs. The company commits to covering grid infrastructure costs, procuring new power generation, and implementing grid optimization and curtailment systems. The policy also includes investments in local communities and support for federal energy and permitting policies.
