Anthropic announced a policy commitment to address the electricity cost impacts of its data center expansion on American consumers. The company will cover grid infrastructure costs, procure new power generation, and invest in curtailment systems to minimize price increases. This is positioned as a responsible approach to supporting AI infrastructure development while protecting ratepayers.
