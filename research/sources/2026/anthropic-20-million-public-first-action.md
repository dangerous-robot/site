Anthropic's $20 million contribution to Public First Action supports efforts to develop bipartisan AI policy including model transparency requirements, federal governance frameworks, and export controls on AI technology. The organization aims to mobilize public and political support for AI safeguards as the technology advances rapidly. Public First Action is led by Republican and Democratic strategists working across party lines on shared policy priorities.
