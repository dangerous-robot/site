The article, published on AI Magazine’s news section, details Anthropic’s four‑pronged commitment to mitigate the electricity and grid impacts of its expanding AI data centre portfolio in the United States. It outlines how the company will cover grid upgrade costs, support new power generation, invest in curtailment and optimisation tools, and advocate for broader energy policy reforms. Anthropic positions these actions as a proactive step toward responsible AI infrastructure development.
