The article details OpenAI's complex ownership structure, including its nonprofit foundation overseeing a commercial arm, key founding figures like Sam Altman and former board member Elon Musk, and recent strategic acquisitions of companies like Statsig, Neptune.ai, and hardware design firm io. It provides a comprehensive overview of how influence is distributed across multiple stakeholders rather than concentrated in a single owner.
