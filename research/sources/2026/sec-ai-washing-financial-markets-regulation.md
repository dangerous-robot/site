This legal analysis by Maxwell Velikodny examines the growing problem of AI-washing in financial services, where firms misrepresent or exaggerate their AI capabilities to attract investors. The article argues for stronger SEC enforcement using existing securities laws, particularly Rule 10b-5 and the Investment Advisers Act, to combat these misleading claims that undermine market integrity.
