This report evaluates Anthropic's transparency practices for their Claude 4 models (Opus 4 and Sonnet 4) against 35 disclosure indicators covering data acquisition, processing, compute resources, and model development. The assessment reveals limited public disclosure across most technical specifications, with Anthropic frequently citing proprietary concerns and competitive advantages as reasons for non-disclosure.
