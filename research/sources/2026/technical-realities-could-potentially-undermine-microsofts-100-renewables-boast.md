Microsoft has contracted around 40GW of renewable energy capacity under power purchase agreements across 26 countries, with 19GW currently operational. The capacity will power its expansion of data centres and AI operations, for which the company plans to invest US$80 billion this year. However, experts raise concerns about the feasibility of meeting growing data centre demand with renewables alone and the reliance on carbon capture technology.
