- The news deals a potentially major setback to the fledgling carbon removal industry,

  early demand.

  projects or when it might resume purchases in the future.

Microsoft's decision to pause carbon removal purchases is a significant development for the carbon removal industry. The company has been a major buyer of carbon removal credits, supporting various technologies and setting industry standards. This pause could impact the industry's growth and its ability to meet climate goals.
