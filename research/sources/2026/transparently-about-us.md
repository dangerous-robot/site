Transparently is a fintech company founded in 2022 by Hamish Macalister (PhD in finance, 30+ years finance experience) and Mauro Sauco (former Google Technical Director). The company develops AI-powered tools to detect accounting fraud and manipulation, with their first platform launching in September 2022.
