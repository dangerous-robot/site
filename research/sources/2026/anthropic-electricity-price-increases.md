Anthropic announces a policy commitment to offset electricity cost impacts of data center expansion on American consumers. The commitment includes covering 100% of grid infrastructure costs for interconnection, procuring new power generation, deploying curtailment systems, and investing in local communities. Anthropic also advocates for federal policies supporting permitting reform and faster grid development.
