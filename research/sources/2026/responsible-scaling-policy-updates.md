This page documents Anthropic's Responsible Scaling Policy (RSP), first released in September 2023 and continuously updated. Version 3.1 (effective April 2, 2026) includes clarifications to AI R&D capability thresholds and confirmations about Anthropic's ability to pause development independently. The page also outlines planned ASL-3 deployment and security safeguards, including multi-layered defense strategies with real-time classifiers, asynchronous monitoring, and post-hoc jailbreak detection for models reaching advanced capability thresholds.
