Claude Mythos Preview is Anthropic's unreleased frontier model announced April 7, 2026, accessible only through Project Glasswing, a partnership program limited to 12 founding organizations and approximately 40 vetted critical-infrastructure operators. The model demonstrates significant capability gaps relative to Claude Opus 4.7 across benchmarks, particularly in cybersecurity where it autonomously discovered thousands of zero-day vulnerabilities including unpatched flaws in OpenBSD, FreeBSD, and Firefox. Anthropic withholds public release, stating the cyber-offense capability outpaces current safeguards and serving Glasswing partners a defensive head-start before broader distribution, with Opus 4.7 serving as the live test vehicle for new cybersecurity measures.
