This page documents Google's comprehensive approach to AI safety and responsibility, covering multiple domains including automated red teaming of Gemini models, content moderation with human reviewers, privacy protections, and security frameworks. Google details specific tools like SynthID for watermarking AI-generated content, Private AI Compute for on-device privacy, and the Frontier Safety Framework for identifying potential harms from advanced AI capabilities. The company emphasizes ecosystem-wide efforts through open sharing of safety frameworks and collaboration with NGOs, academics, and industry partners.
