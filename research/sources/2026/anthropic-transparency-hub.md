This is Anthropic's official transparency resource documenting their Claude model family, including technical specifications, safety evaluations, and risk assessments. The hub contains model reports, system trust and reporting information, and voluntary commitments, with the most recent update dated February 20, 2026. It includes detailed evaluation results under Anthropic's Responsible Scaling Policy framework across multiple risk domains including CBRN, cybersecurity, and autonomous AI R&D capabilities.
