This UNESCO report examines how companies are implementing responsible AI practices by analyzing the largest global dataset of corporate AI disclosures. The study focuses on the gap between AI adoption speed and governance evolution, emphasizing the need for clearer accountability mechanisms in corporate AI deployment.
