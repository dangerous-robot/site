This transparency report assesses Google's disclosure practices for Gemini 2.5 Pro across 35 indicators covering data acquisition, processing, compute usage, and model development. Google received low scores on most indicators, often citing security concerns and competitive disadvantages as reasons for non-disclosure. The report evaluates transparency in areas including data sources, synthetic data generation, compute infrastructure, and organizational structure.
