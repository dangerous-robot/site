- Risk Reports will provide detailed information on the safety profile of our models

- The combination of (a) the zone of ambiguity muddling the public case for risk,

  our current RSP.

This is Anthropic's updated voluntary framework for managing catastrophic AI risks, reflecting two years of experience with their previous Responsible Scaling Policy. The document introduces new transparency measures including Risk Reports and external review processes, while acknowledging challenges in translating capability thresholds into clear policy action. It separates unilateral company commitments from broader industry recommendations.
