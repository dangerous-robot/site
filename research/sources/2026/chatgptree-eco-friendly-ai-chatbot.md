ChatGPTree positions itself as a sustainable alternative to traditional AI chatbots, offering GPT-4o capabilities while automatically planting trees monthly through partnerships with Evertreen and Veritree. The service has planted 3,950 trees and claims to offset 27.1 million chats per year.
