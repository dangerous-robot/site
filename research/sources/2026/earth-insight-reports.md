This index page showcases Earth Insight's research portfolio documenting threats to critical ecosystems from fossil fuel and mining expansion. The reports cover three major tropical forest basins (Amazon, Congo, Mekong-Borneo) and marine biodiversity hotspots, with particular focus on impacts to Indigenous Peoples and local communities.
