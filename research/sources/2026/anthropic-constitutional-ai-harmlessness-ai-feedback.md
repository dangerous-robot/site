This technical report introduces Constitutional AI, a novel approach to AI alignment that aims to train helpful and harmless AI systems. The method uses a constitution (set of principles) to guide AI behavior and employs AI feedback rather than human feedback to evaluate and improve outputs. The research demonstrates a promising direction for scaling AI alignment techniques.
