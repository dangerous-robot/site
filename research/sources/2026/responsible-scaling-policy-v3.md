Anthropic released the third version of its Responsible Scaling Policy in February 2026, an update to its voluntary framework for managing AI risks. The new version separates Anthropic's unilateral safety commitments from its industry-wide recommendations, and introduces Frontier Safety Roadmaps as public goals and Risk Reports with external review to improve transparency. The policy reflects two-and-a-half years of experience implementing earlier versions and acknowledges structural challenges in creating multilateral action for higher-capability AI systems.
