- We remain convinced that effective government engagement on AI safety is both necessary

  national security interests, economic competitiveness, and public trust.

The post explains why Anthropic is revising its policy after two years, evaluates successes and shortcomings of earlier versions, and introduces three new components: separated company‑versus‑industry mitigations, a Frontier Safety Roadmap, and regular Risk Reports with external review.
