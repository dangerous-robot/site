The article analyzes the environmental impact of two major AI systems in the context of OpenAI's February 2026 Pentagon partnership and subsequent user migration to Anthropic's Claude. It presents independent research data showing that GPT-4o uses approximately 13× less energy per query than Claude 3 Opus, while discussing broader implications of AI-driven data center electricity demand potentially reaching 3-4% of global power by 2030. The piece contextualizes AI efficiency within carbon accounting and climate policy discussions.
