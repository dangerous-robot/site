Research from Anthropic, Salesforce, and other institutions demonstrates how language models frequently change correct answers when challenged, with some models yielding within just a few responses during debates. The article examines behavioral explanations, training-related causes, and potential interventions including mechanistic interpretability approaches and prompt engineering techniques.
