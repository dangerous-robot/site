Google.org serves as Google's philanthropic arm, connecting nonprofits to funding and resources. The site highlights major initiatives across three strategic focus areas: Knowledge, Skills & Learning; Scientific Progress; and Stronger Communities. Notable programs include Project Greenlight for reducing traffic congestion, AI Collaborative for wildfire tracking, and CiviForm for improving government service delivery.
