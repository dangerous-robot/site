The article examines the controversial transition of OpenAI from a nonprofit research lab to a hybrid structure where its foundation now holds a $180 billion stake in the company. Despite early grants totaling $40.5 million to community organizations, critics argue the foundation serves as a distraction from OpenAI's departure from its original safety-focused mission.
