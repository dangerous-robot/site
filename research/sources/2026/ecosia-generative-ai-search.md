This comprehensive help article explains Ecosia's approach to integrating generative AI while maintaining their environmental and privacy commitments. The documentation covers both AI Overviews powered by Mistral-small-3.2 and AI Chat using OpenAI's GPT-4.1 mini, emphasizing energy efficiency and user control.
