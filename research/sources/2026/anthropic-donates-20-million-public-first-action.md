Anthropic announced a $20 million contribution to Public First Action, established to advance bipartisan AI policy and public education. The organization prioritizes AI model transparency, federal governance frameworks, strategic export controls on AI chips, and targeted regulation of AI-enabled biological weapons and cyberattacks. Anthropic frames the donation as supporting public interest governance rather than advancing the company's own interests.
