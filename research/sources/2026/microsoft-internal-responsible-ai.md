The post describes the organizational structure used to govern AI development at Microsoft, specifically the role of the Office of Responsible AI (ORA) and the companywide Responsible AI Council. It outlines six core principles: fairness, privacy and security, reliability and safety, inclusiveness, transparency, and accountability.
