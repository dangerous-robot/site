The article details how Microsoft achieved its renewable energy goal through various projects, including solar farms in Illinois, hydroelectric plants in West Virginia, and wind farms in Brazil. These projects not only contribute to renewable energy but also benefit local communities through educational initiatives, job creation, and infrastructure improvements.
