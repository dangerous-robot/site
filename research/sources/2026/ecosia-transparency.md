  monthly financial reports, annual regeneration reports, and compliance with UK and

key_quotes:
- Each month, we publish a financial report that shows how much money we make and
  how much we invest in planting trees.
- The trees we plant are not used to offset emissions. We focus on avoiding emissions,
  offsetting unavoidable ones with high-quality carbon credits, and producing twice
  as much renewable energy as needed for Ecosia searches.
- As of April 2025, Ecosia's UK monthly active users number does not meet the threshold
  set out by Ofcom for Category 2A regulated search services.

This documentation outlines Ecosia's transparency framework, covering their monthly financial reporting system and annual regeneration reports that detail environmental impact. It also addresses regulatory compliance for digital safety standards in the UK and EU.
