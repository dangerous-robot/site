Earth Insight presents their organizational impact through rapid threat assessments, policy engagement with governments, and research partnerships. They have developed mapping tools analyzing fossil fuel threats to tropical forests across multiple continents and supported policy measures like Colombia's Amazon oil ban announcement at COP 30.
