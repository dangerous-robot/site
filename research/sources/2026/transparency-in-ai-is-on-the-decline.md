The article summarizes findings from the 2025 Foundation Model Transparency Index, a collaborative effort by researchers from Stanford, Berkeley, Princeton, and MIT. It highlights a downward trend in transparency scores from 2024 to 2025 and distinguishes between 'openness' (weight availability) and 'transparency' (disclosure of practices).
