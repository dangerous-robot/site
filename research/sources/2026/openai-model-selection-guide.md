This documentation provides practical guidance for selecting OpenAI models, emphasizing a two-step approach: first optimizing for accuracy, then reducing cost and latency. It includes a detailed example of building a fake news classifier that achieved 91.5% accuracy while reducing costs by 98% through fine-tuning a smaller model.
