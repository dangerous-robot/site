The article traces OpenAI's organizational restructuring from its 2015 founding as a nonprofit through multiple iterations of hybrid profit/nonprofit structures. It examines the company's recent transformation to a public benefit corporation model and details its evolving grantmaking activities, including $40.5 million in grants through its "People First AI Fund."
