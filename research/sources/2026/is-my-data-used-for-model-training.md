This documentation page from Anthropic's Privacy Center outlines the conditions under which user data from Claude products (Free, Pro, Max) is used for model training. Data usage requires explicit user consent for Model Improvement, with exceptions for safety review and trusted tester programs. Incognito chats are explicitly excluded from model training regardless of privacy settings.
