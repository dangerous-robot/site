The article estimates Claude's daily energy consumption at approximately 12.76 MWh/day (~4.66 GWh/year) and water usage at ~24,239 litres/day based on published inference benchmarks and industry standard metrics. Key uncertainties acknowledged include actual prompt volumes and the location and cooling methods of data centres hosting Claude. The author emphasizes that without standardized disclosure from AI companies, energy comparisons remain speculative and difficult to verify.
