This article provides order-of-magnitude estimates of Anthropic's Claude AI energy and water consumption by combining published inference benchmarks with third-party user data. The analysis uses a Claude 3 Opus benchmark of 4.05 Wh per ~400-token exchange and assumes 18.9 million monthly active users sending five prompts daily to estimate roughly 12.76 MWh of daily electricity consumption and 24,239 litres of daily water consumption. The authors emphasize uncertainty in their calculations due to unknown prompt volumes and facility-specific cooling efficiency variations.
