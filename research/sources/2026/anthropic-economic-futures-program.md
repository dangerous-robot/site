Anthropic's Economic Futures program consists of three interconnected pillars: catalyzing independent research through grants and partnerships; facilitating evidence-based policy development through symposia; and expanding economic measurement via the Anthropic Economic Index. The program offers Research Awards ($10,000-$50,000) for empirical AI economic research and hosts policy symposia in collaboration with Georgetown University and the London School of Economics. Proposals addressing labor transitions, productivity effects, fiscal policy, and international impacts are actively solicited.
