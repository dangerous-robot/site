Anthropic released Claude Opus 4.6 on February 5, 2026, positioning it as the company's smartest model to date. The model features a 1M token context window, improved agentic and reasoning capabilities, and new product integrations including Claude Code (with agent teams), Claude in Excel, and Claude in PowerPoint. Benchmarking shows Opus 4.6 outperforms competitors on tasks including Terminal-Bench 2.0, GDPval-AA, and Humanity's Last Exam, with comparable safety profiles to its predecessor.
