Anthropic's Economic Futures program consists of three interconnected pillars: catalyzing independent research through grants and partnerships, facilitating policy development via symposia, and expanding economic measurement through the Anthropic Economic Index. The program offers multiple pathways for participation, including research awards ($10,000–$50,000), policy symposia in Washington D.C. and London, and strategic partnerships with research institutions. The initiative addresses research questions spanning labor market transitions, human-AI complementarity, productivity effects, and policy responses to AI-driven economic changes.
