MindStudio published this blog post explaining Anthropic's approach to handling Claude Mythos, an internal frontier model that exceeds public safety thresholds defined in the company's Responsible Scaling Policy (RSP). The article details the AI Safety Level (ASL) framework that classifies models by capability and required safety measures, positioning Mythos at ASL-3 or beyond due to its reported strengths in autonomous reasoning, scientific domain knowledge, and persuasion. Project Glasswing represents Anthropic's methodology for evaluating such models through controlled red-teaming and capability elicitation studies before potential deployment.
