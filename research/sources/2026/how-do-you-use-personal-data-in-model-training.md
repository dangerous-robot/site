This is documentation from Anthropic's Privacy Center explaining how personal data is handled in Claude's model training. It describes data collection sources, privacy safeguards including Constitutional AI principles, and user controls for opting in or out of having their conversations used for model improvement. The article clarifies that personal data is incidentally included rather than actively targeted, and that Claude is specifically trained not to disclose such data.
