The announcement came amid growing political pressure on AI firms to mitigate their impact on electricity rates. Anthropic’s pledge mirrors similar commitments from OpenAI and Microsoft. The article notes that AI data centers accounted for 4.4% of U.S. electricity in 2024, projected to rise to 12% by 2028.
