This is Alphabet Inc.'s investor relations homepage announcing the restructuring of Google under the new parent company Alphabet. The page features a founder letter from Larry Page explaining the rationale for creating the holding company structure, the leadership appointments (Sundar Pichai as Google CEO, Larry Page as Alphabet CEO, and Sergey Brin as President), and the separation of core internet businesses from speculative ventures like Life Sciences and X lab. The announcement also details how existing Google shareholders' shares would automatically convert to Alphabet shares and how the two share classes would continue trading on Nasdaq.
