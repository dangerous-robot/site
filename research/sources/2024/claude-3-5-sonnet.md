Claude 3.5 Sonnet was released on June 21, 2024, as Anthropic's first model in the Claude 3.5 family. The announcement includes introduction of Artifacts, a feature enabling real-time collaborative editing of AI-generated code, documents, and designs on Claude.ai. The model achieved ASL-2 safety ratings and received evaluation from the UK Artificial Intelligence Safety Institute.
