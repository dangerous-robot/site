Anthropic's announcement of the Claude 3 model family on March 4, 2024, introduced three capability tiers designed to balance performance with cost and speed. The models demonstrated improvements over Claude 2.1 across multiple evaluation benchmarks, including MMLU, GPQA, and GSM8K, while introducing new features such as vision capabilities and a 200K token context window. Anthropic classified Claude 3 models at Safety Level 2 under their Responsible Scaling Policy, concluding from red-team evaluations that they present negligible catastrophic risk.
