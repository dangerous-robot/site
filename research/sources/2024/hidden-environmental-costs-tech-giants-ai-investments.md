This analysis by CFA researcher Joo Hee Lee examines how major tech companies like Microsoft, Amazon, and Alphabet handle environmental reporting for AI investments. The piece specifically critiques how corporate partnerships with AI companies like OpenAI and Anthropic may obscure true carbon footprints in sustainability disclosures.
