Microsoft announced a record-breaking renewable energy purchase agreement with Brookfield Asset Management to secure 10.5 gigawatts of new global renewable capacity by 2030. The deal aims to support Microsoft's AI infrastructure expansion while helping the company meet its carbon negativity goal by 2030. BloombergNEF estimates the project will cost $11.5–$17 billion and is nearly eight times larger than any previous corporate power purchase agreement.
