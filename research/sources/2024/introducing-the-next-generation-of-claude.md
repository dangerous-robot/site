The announcement details three models—Claude 3 Haiku, Sonnet, and Opus—each targeting different performance and cost levels. It provides specifications such as a 200K (up to 1M) token context window, pricing per million tokens, and availability via Anthropic’s API, claude.ai, Amazon Bedrock, and upcoming Google Cloud integration.
