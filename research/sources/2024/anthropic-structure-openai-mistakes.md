The article examines how Anthropic structured itself as a public benefit corporation with a Long Term Benefit Trust to balance profit motives with safety considerations, contrasting this approach with OpenAI's failed governance model. It explores whether corporate structures can adequately constrain AI development or if government regulation is necessary.
