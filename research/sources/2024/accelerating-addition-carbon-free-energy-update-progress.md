Microsoft's power purchase agreement with Constellation will enable the restart of an 835 MW nuclear facility in Pennsylvania, adding significant carbon-free electricity to the PJM power grid. This aligns with Microsoft's commitment to becoming carbon negative by 2030 and supports a more sustainable future.
