Anthropic's updated RSP introduces AI Safety Level (ASL) Standards graduated from ASL-1 to higher levels based on model capabilities. The policy defines two key capability thresholds requiring enhanced safeguards: autonomous AI research and development, and chemical/biological/radiological/nuclear weapons assistance. Implementation includes routine capability assessments, safeguard evaluations, and external expert feedback mechanisms.
