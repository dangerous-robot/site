The program offers compensation, research budgets of ~$10,000 per fellow per month, mentorship from senior Anthropic researchers, and community support. Research focus areas include scalable oversight, adversarial robustness, model organisms, interpretability, and AI welfare. Applications closed January 20, 2025, with the first cohort scheduled to begin mid-March 2025.
