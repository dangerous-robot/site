Microsoft's power purchase agreement with Constellation will restart an 835 MW nuclear facility in Pennsylvania, providing a significant supply of net-new, reliable, carbon-free electricity to the PJM power grid. This initiative is part of Microsoft's commitment to becoming carbon negative by 2030 and supporting a more sustainable future.
