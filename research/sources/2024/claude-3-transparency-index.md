This is an official transparency assessment of Anthropic's Claude 3 model by Stanford's Foundation Model Transparency Index (FMTI) from May 2024. The report evaluates 51 disclosure indicators across three categories—upstream (data and development), model architecture, and downstream (deployment and access)—assigning scores for each. Anthropic achieved total scores of 7 in upstream disclosure, 21 in model disclosure, and 23 in downstream disclosure, covering transparency about training data, hardware infrastructure, model capabilities, limitations, and safety mitigations.
