Microsoft's Q2 2024 earnings report showcases strong financial performance with revenue growth and increased earnings per share. The report also discusses Microsoft's environmental commitments, including goals to become carbon negative by 2030 and offset all carbon dioxide emissions since 1975 by 2050. The company faces challenges in reducing Scope 1, 2, and 3 emissions, with Scope 3 emissions accounting for over 96% of its total emissions.
