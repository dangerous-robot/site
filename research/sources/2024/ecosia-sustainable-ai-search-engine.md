Forbes interview discusses Ecosia's approach to integrating AI while maintaining its environmental mission, including the company's partnership with French search engine Qwant to develop an independent search index. The article covers Ecosia's business model of using ad revenue to fund tree planting projects and the environmental challenges posed by energy-intensive AI models.
