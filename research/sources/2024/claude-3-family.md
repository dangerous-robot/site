Anthropic's March 2024 announcement introducing the Claude 3 family of language models across three capability tiers. The models include vision capabilities, support for inputs up to 1 million tokens (initially 200K context window), and improvements in accuracy, reasoning, and multi-step instruction following. Opus and Sonnet were made available through the Claude API and claude.ai, while Haiku became available later.
