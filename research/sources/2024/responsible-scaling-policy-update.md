Anthropic's updated RSP (released October 15, 2024) introduces AI Safety Level Standards (ASL Standards) that scale with model capabilities, drawing inspiration from biosafety frameworks. The policy defines two key capability thresholds triggering enhanced safeguards: autonomous AI research capabilities and CBRN weapons assistance. The update reflects one year of implementation experience and incorporates lessons from instances where the company identified gaps in policy adherence.
