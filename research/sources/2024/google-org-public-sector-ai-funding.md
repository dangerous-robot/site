Google.org allocated $10 million to the Partnership for Public Service to establish the Center for Federal AI and $5 million to InnovateUS to expand AI training for state and local government workers. The funding is part of Google.org's $75 million AI Opportunity Fund aimed at helping Americans develop essential AI skills.
