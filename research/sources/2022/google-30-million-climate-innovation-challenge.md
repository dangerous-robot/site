Google's Chief Sustainability Officer Kate Brandt announces a new funding initiative targeting nonprofits and social enterprises developing climate solutions. The program builds on Google's previous climate innovation funding in Europe and Asia-Pacific, seeking to accelerate development of tools for advocates, policymakers, businesses and individuals.
