The article discusses how Microsoft's Environmental Credit Service aims to address challenges in the carbon credit market, such as inconsistent quality standards and siloed credit registries. The service provides a common infrastructure to improve transparency and track the provenance of carbon credits, benefiting project developers, verifiers, registries, and marketplace participants.
