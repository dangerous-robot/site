Kate Brandt, Google's Chief Sustainability Officer, outlines the company's climate commitment and announces a $30 million initiative supporting nonprofits developing climate solutions using AI, machine learning, and open data. Six selected projects will receive $5 million each plus in-kind technical support. The initiative builds on Google's existing climate tools like Environmental Insights Explorer and Carbon Footprint.
