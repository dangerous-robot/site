The 2022 Impact Challenge selected projects from World Resources Institute, Woodwell Climate Research Center, The New School, World Wildlife Fund, Global Methane Hub, and Rewiring America. Each recipient received $5M in funding plus access to Google's technical expertise and products. The initiative focused on accelerating technological advances in climate information and action.
